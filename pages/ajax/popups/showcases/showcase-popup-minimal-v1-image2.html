Amazon Delivery Concept

Semester 2 2017 Solo Project

This was a project that I created for an elective in my second semester of school. The project's goal was for students to create a concept or model of an invention that could be beneficial to society. I decided that I would take two technologies/services that already exist and put them together to create a service of the future. My idea was to create an application that would allow users to buy groceries from a fleet of refrigerated self-driving trucks.

Each truck would carry basic goods as well as less sought-after goods such as grenadine or almond milk. The idea was that no one truck would be carrying the exact same goods because no trucks are big enough to do that. When customers order groceries the trucks would use AI to determine 1. Which one of them carries the most goods that the customer is looking for and 2. which truck is closest to the user. The trucks would use machine learning and hive technology to determine what the most efficient way would be to deliver the users groceries.

The idea is that if this technology was implemented properly there would be no need for grocery stores. All groceries would be inside of an autonomous fleet of trucks.
