Purple Fitness Tracker

Information Architecture:
Solo Project

The goal of this project was to create an application mockup that would have an intuitive hierarchy of information/data. I decided to create a fitness tracking application.

The app is an exercise history manager that helps people keep their workout schedule on track. It is aimed towards people who would like to be more serious about going to the gym. It is designed to allow people to track what day it is at the gym (shoulders, chest, arms, leg, rest, etc.) as well as the exercises that they would like to complete each day.
This app is designed to make the lives of people who want to work out easier. It helps smooth out the process of working out by allowing its users to log important information seamlessly.
