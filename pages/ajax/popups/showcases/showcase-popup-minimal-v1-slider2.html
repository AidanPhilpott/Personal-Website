Smartane Project

2017-Present Team Project

Role:
Creative Director

I am a part of a startup called SmartCane.

I am the lead UX designer and one of the graphic designers for SmartCane.The SmartCane is a modern device for orientation and mobility for the visually impaired, blind and deafblind. It is an enhanced white cane that notifies users when an object is in their path and helps users navigate to a certain location through tactile output in the form of vibrations. The we are also working on some new features such as an emergency button, and AI integration.

SmartCane is in its early stages but our team has entered and won several hackathons and pitch competitions. We are using the resources provided by our wins to help bring our idea to market in the near future. Smartcane has recieved over $70,000 in winnings over last year.

The SmartCane team is led by the amazing Riya Karumanchi who laid the foundations for this project when she was in grade 8.

  • Project Goal

    To bring a modern device for orientation and mobility for the visually impaired, blind and deafblind to market.

    In the future, I will be working with the blind and deaf-blind communities to help test the cane that we have been working on.
