Orange Gate

Summer Placement 2018
Part Time 2018

I worked at Spider as a junior designer. I helped them with graphic design and brainstorming for advertisement ideas.

This design was for one of our client's winter 2017 advertisement campaigns. They wanted us to create a nice winter-themed advertisement that incorporated their company in some way. During the company pitches, I came up with the idea to create bells out of wine glasses. Spider loved the idea and ended up using my mockups in their pitch deck. The client liked the idea as well and the idea was refined by Spider's lead designer to create the final product that is shown above.

  • My Role in this design

    I helped with the brainstorming and conceptualizing of this idea. I also did all of the initial mockups for the winter-themed pitch. In the end, some of the ideas that I was working on were chosen to be refined and eventually, this advertisement was finalized.

  • Roadblocks

    We had a couple of roadblocks along the way. One problem was that we were not able to show alcohol in a glass because that is against the law. Others included not being able to use wreaths and corks in our design because they were used the previous year. We also had to create a design that was not only focused on Christmas but rather the holidays as a whole.
